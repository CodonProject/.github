# Hi there. Welcome to Codon Project. 👋

## About Us
Codon Project is an open-source research initiative focused on Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and computational consciousness modeling. We aim to explore the architectures and mechanisms behind machine intelligence and self-awareness through practical development and experimental verification.

## Area of Focus
- **Large Language Models (LLM):** Research and development of custom-built LLMs.
- **Datasets:** Open-source datasets designed for cognitive research and complex reasoning tasks.
- **Proof of Concept (PoC):** Experimental prototypes and implementations of advanced intelligent systems.
